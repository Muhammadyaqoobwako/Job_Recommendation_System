How the AI works

Data-driven match

We combine your skills, interests and experience with a curated career database and job-market trends. Then an AI model ranks career matches by fit and potential.

Hybrid recommendation

Content-based matching (skills ↔ career descriptions) plus collaborative signals from users with similar profiles — you get better results than static questionnaires.

Privacy & fairness

No personally-identifiable data is shared. Recommendations focus on skills, interests and career fit.
